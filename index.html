Hello my name is Matt. Being creative and solving new challenges are what I enjoy the most in design. Whether its drawing, working in CAD, or talking to users, I enjoy exploring new ways to be creative and love a good challenge.

I am obsessed with trends in technology In my free time I like to 3D print prototypes, wood work, and build computers.
